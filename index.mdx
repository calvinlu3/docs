OncoKB provides structured, evidence-based annotation for somatic and germline genomic alterations in cancer. Through its REST API, you can annotate mutations by protein change, genomic coordinates, or HGVS notation, and receive curated therapeutic, diagnostic, and prognostic levels of evidence tied to specific tumor types.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get access">
    Register at [oncokb.org](https://www.oncokb.org) and obtain an API token. Public endpoints are available without a token; premium endpoints require one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate your requests">
    Include your token as a Bearer token in the `Authorization` header of every API request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit a variant query">
    Call any annotation endpoint — by protein change, genomic change, HGVSg, CNA, or structural variant — and receive a structured JSON response.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interpret the response">
    The response includes oncogenicity, mutation effect, therapeutic level, treatment options, and supporting literature — all tied to the specific tumor type you queried.
  </Step>
</Steps>
